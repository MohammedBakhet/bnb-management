Most Beautiful House in the World 1: Dracula Palace, Romania 

Known as Bran Palace but famous as Dracula Palace in the rest of the world, it is a national monument and a very important landmark in Romania. Around 1920, the Brand Castle was the official seat of the Kingdom of Romania. The gorgeous castle is located about 2500 ft. above sea level and is a big touristic attraction. The Palace is surrounded by luscious green forests and also houses a museum. The fun fact is Bram Stoker, who was an Irish writer is best remembered for his popular horror novel, Dracula, never visited Transylvania in his entire life, and the Bran castle got its name Dracula palace only because this fortress matches the description of Stokerâ€™s characterâ€™s castle in the novel.

2. Updown Court, UK

It is one of the biggest and the most beautiful homes in the world which is privately owned. Not very far from London, it is situated on a secluded estate. The Updown Court is set in an expansive 58 acres of forested land in Windlesham. This magnificent four-storey mansion in cream colour consists of 103 rooms which include 24 en-suite bedrooms, a security lodge, 5 pools, a cinema hall, a garage for luxury vehicles, a helipad, and 2 guest houses. 

This pristine white building is surrounded by beautiful lush greenery with a minimum crowd surrounding it. An ultimate paradise for luxurious yet peaceful living. 

Architectural marvel

3. Antilia, India - One of the Most Beautiful Houses in the World

The personal skyscraper in the midst of Mumbai city is home to the Ambani family - one of the wealthiest business names in the world. The entire building plan is based on Vastu Shastra, as the family has believed in it for generations. This magnificent mansion's architectural design has been inspired by the lotus and the sun. It has 27 floors, and the top six floors of the building are entirely designated as residential space. In contrast, the Ambani's house has several guest rooms, a big temple, an ice-cream parlour, a private movie theatre to accommodate about 50 people, and a salon. 

Apart from that, Antilia also has 3 helipads, air traffic control, a 168 car garage, a ballroom, 9 elevators, a theatre, a terrace garden, swimming pools, a spa, a health centre, a temple and also a snow room!

4. Villa La Leopolda, France 

Constructed in 1902 by the Belgian King, Leopold II, it truly deserves its place in the list of most beautiful houses in the world list. Located at the ever-so-heavenly French Riviera, its beauty is unmatched. The Villa La Leopolda is built on land that was once owned by the Kings. The property has served as a beautiful backdrop in several notable movies, including one film by Hitchcock. The villa is built on sprawling 18 acres of land and offers a breathtaking view overlooking the beautiful verdant valley.

Though in comparison to other same-league properties, it has only 11 bedrooms and 14 bathrooms, it compensates well with a beautiful green cover which requires 50 gardeners' efforts to maintain. 

French Riviera setting

Most Beautiful House in the World 5: South Coogee, Australia 

Beautiful property for people who live and love the blue water. One of the levels of the house, considering its proximity to the sea, is a large swimming pool. It has a kitchen, five bedrooms and a couple of living rooms. 

The scheme is so broad and ambitious that it has been under construction for many years for this "summer residence".

6. Versailles - Florida, US

This is one of the biggest and the most beautiful houses in the world. This 80,000 sq ft house is owned by the Westgate resortâ€™s owner. It is one of the largest properties owned by a family, inspired by the Palace of Versailles in France. Everything about this palace is extraordinary. Versailles in Florida is built on a sprawling 10-acre waterfront estate overlooking the picturesque Lake Butler. The palace has a man-made hill that was constructed for the castle to be situated on. 

Its building began in 2004 and lasted until 2019 and consists of 14 bedrooms, 11 kitchens, a wine cellar, two tennis courts, and outdoor and indoor pools.

 

Touch of French

7. The Manor, California - Most Beautiful House in the World 

Designed in 1988 for the TV producer Aaron Spelling, it is Los Angeles County's largest home. It was initially purchased in 2011 for $85 million but was later put into the market for two years with an asking price of $150 million. 

In June 2019, The Manor sold for $119.7 million to an unknown bidder; the transaction set a new record as the highest sale in California history. 

The Manor is a French chateau-style mansion of 56,500 square feet with 123 rooms (including 27 bathrooms and 14 bedrooms) and sits on 5 acres of land.

8. Starwood Estate, Aspen 

Another luxury property in the US that promises the best view of natureâ€™s beauty and glamour. This property spreads across 30 acres with 5 bedrooms and 8 bathrooms with an unobstructed view of the rocky mountains.

Most Beautiful House in the World 10: One Hyde Park Penthouse, UK 

This luxury penthouse is estimated at $160 million and is located in the most expensive locality of London, i.e. Knightsbridge. It was completed in 2009 as a very ambitious project in collaboration with  Rogers Stirk Harbour + Partners, and an interior design by the Candy brothers. 

It has some very unique features like heated marble floors, walk-in wardrobes, a 21-meter pool, a screening room, and an extensive library. Mandarin hotel has provided 24 hr security, maid service, a dry cleaning room, and a wine store! 

It is advertised as a luxury smart home and has almost 80 residences. It is home to billionaires, celebrities, and businessmen. 

The ultimate beauty

11. Villa Malaparte, Italy

Villa Malaparte is a marvellous island property designed by the Italian architect Adalberto Libera in 1943. It is located on the Capri island in Italy. An Italian writer, Curzio Malapatre, commissioned this property as a relaxing and calm haven where he could write in peace with focus. No denying the fact that Italy is chockful of stunning beaches, and this house stands on a cliff overlooking the sea. It can only be accessed by foot through a steep staircase from the main road. 

This house's unique, striking design has set an iconic example of modernist architecture. It is made of local stone and has a curved roof merging with the surrounding landscape. The property's facade has a large red stripe running around the perimeter and complementing the natural stone.

12. The Round House, England - Most Beautiful House in the World 

Also called The Toll House, The Round House is a unique residential property located in Langton Green close to Tunbridge Wells, England. This architectural delight was designed by the British architect James Wright in 1968. It has a unique circular shape, giving it a one-of-a-kind appearance amidst the magnificence of the landscape. 

This house is constructed from reinforced concrete and features a large domed roof, which is its major highlight and provides ample natural light. The property comprises a spacious, open living area, kitchen and bedrooms settled across the central circular shape. Further, the house has several balconies and terraces presenting an incredible view of the surrounding countryside.


The domed roof is a drawing feature of this house

13. Sky Garden, Singapore 

The Sky Garden, a residential property located in Singapore, was completed in 2011. An iconic landmark in the city, the house is built on stilts and offers a stunning view of the city's landscapes through its cantilevered glass facade. The house enjoys an abundant inflow of natural lights with floor-to-ceiling windows and a spacious open layout. 

The house was designed by Guz Architects, who are known for their unique ideas. The Sky Garden, Singapore, is developed with natural greenery all around, and the glass windows blur the lines between the outdoors and indoors. 

The house stands in the city as an architectural marvel and is frequently visited by people who admire its modern design and futuristic elements. 

Whether you step outside of the house or visit its rooftop for a walk, you can witness the surrounding fauna from all corners. 

Sky Garden, Singapore has lush greenery around and an impressive architecture (Source: Pinterest)

14. Dupli Casa, Ludwigsburg, Germany

Dupli Casa is a futuristic private residence with mind-blowing architecture and sprawling green space. It is strategically located in Ludwigsburg, Germany. 

A dramatic fusion of sleek design and spectacular aesthetics, this structure is constructed on the footprint of a previous house. The white smooth exterior creates a peaceful, calming atmosphere whereas the elongated glass walls and terraces with black borders inject ultimate luxury to the setting. What draws the most about this architectural wonder is the magnificent visual scenery of the countryside and the Neckar River in the vicinity. 

The house features three floors with huge living spaces, bedrooms and open staircases. The layout is a representation of simplicity, openness and comfort. The entire aura of this property is welcoming and relaxing.


The innovative architecture with a visual scenery (Source: Pinterest)

5 Most Beautiful Places to Live in the World

Seol, Korea

One of the biggest cities in Korea, Seoul is a stunning place with vibrant culture and incredible mountains around. The city is renowned for its spicy food and fantastic nightlife across the globe.

Cape Town, South Africa

Located between the ocean and the mountains, Cape Town is counted among the most liveable cities in Africa. While its natural beauty is a treat for sore eyes, the plenty of options for food and nightlife appeal to tourists and residents alike.

Edinburgh, Scotland

One of the loveliest places to live in the UK, Edinburgh has a unique Scottish charm that appeals to all. With a vibrant city centre and castle on the hill, the city has a lot to offer to its residents and tourists.

Santiago, Chile

Located amid beaches and snow-capped mountains, Santiago is one of the world's most beautiful places to live. It is one of South America's safest and most reasonably priced cities, with gorgeous natural beauty and impressive architecture.

Auckland, New Zealand

Auckland is loved for its dramatic landscape in New Zealand. The city offers gorgeous views and some of the best food in the country at reasonable prices. Besides, the relaxed Kiwi lifestyle draws people from different parts of the world.
